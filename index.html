Torrington Deserves Better

Our beloved skatepark is in danger of being removed and/or stored indefinitely. Now that the warmer weather is here we simply cannot let the one place we have to safely enjoy action sports go to waste. It is imperative that all of us local enthusiasts band together, sign the petition, and ensure our voices are heard on this matter.

Sign the Petition

Background

Residents of the city recently approved a nearly $200 million dollar budget to rebuild Torrington High School, which is where the skatepark is located. Unfortunately, plans were not made in time to relocate the skatepark during the construction activities, and residents will potentially be deprived access to the ramps during the entire summer of 2022. If the park closes, we will lose our safe place to skate and BMX.

Sign the petition

Reach out

Not only is this unacceptable, it is high time the city considers using a portion of that budget to rectify past transgressions and build a proper professional level freestyle venue for area residents to enjoy.
